beaches in Sicily are one of the most beautiful things about the island! A few of the best beaches are Mondello, Guidaloca and Lo Zingaro, San Vito Lo Capo, the Egadi Islands, Selinunte and the Belice Nature Reserve, Torre Salsa, Cava d'Aliga, Donnalucata, Sampieri, Marina di Modica and Marina di Ragusa, up through Marzamemi to the Vendicari Nature Reserve, La Pineta del Gelsomineto, Fontane Bianche, Arenella and the coast around Siracusa. The thing I personally love about these beaches is the view and the fact that they're sand beaches.
